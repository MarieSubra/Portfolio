RTE – 7-week internship

Description : RTE is a French company that transmits high and very high voltage electricity throughout the country.

Mission : I studied the short-circuit current of equipment used to connect power lines to each other. To do this, I used AutoCAD software and an SAP product. This enabled the RTE database to be updated.

Skills developed : Autonomy, rigour, communication.
