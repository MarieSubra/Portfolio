Zeer Pot Project

This is a modelling of a zeer pot we made to illustrate our report

During my two years of preparatory classes, I worked for four months with a team of four people on a thermodynamic project : the zeer pot. It is a refrigerator that keeps food cool through evaporation. It is used in dry and hot developing countries because it works without electricity and is economical. We first studied the theoretical functioning of the zeer pot. Then we set up experiments to optimise its use.

We also made a tutorial to create your own zeer pot with objects that are easy to find. It was also with this zeer pot that we carried out our experiments.
