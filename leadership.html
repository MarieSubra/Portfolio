Civic Engagement & Leadership

Completed Projects

Local Market Treasure Hunt

I designed an educational treasure hunt on a market in the Pyrenees. The aim was to highlight local products, support farmers and breeders, and raise awareness on pastoralism-related issues such as the presence of bears in the mountains.

Transhumance Association in Bethmale

I am an active member of the Bethmale Transhumance Association, which helps move horses to high mountain pastures during the summer season. This event promotes traditional practices and strengthens the link between humans, animals, and nature.

Upcoming Projects

Solidarity Trip to Togo

Next summer, I will take part in a 5-week solidarity trip to Togo. The mission’s goal is to help and supervise children living in precarious conditions, while discovering a new culture and promoting educational development.
