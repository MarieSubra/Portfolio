Civic Engagement

Completed Projects

Local Market : "Les pastorales de Guzet"

Every year, I help organise a market in the mountains that promotes local products and the work of farmers and breeders. This market is an opportunity to bring the region to life and raise its profile. I help set up the marquees, serve the evening meal and write and distribute the songbooks. I also organise a treasure hunt for children so that they can discover the market in a fun way with riddles and questions to ask the traders.

Transhumance Association in Bethmale

Every year, farmers take their livestock up into the mountains for the summer: this practice allows them to make optimal use of the natural resources available throughout the year. The herds feed on the green grass of the mountains, thus avoiding overgrazing. Transhumance also serves to pass on traditional knowledge and boosts the local economy. In this association, I help to accompany the horses from the farmer's premises to the mountainous area where they will spend the summer. I also help to distribute meals during the event.

Future Projects

Solidarity Trip to Togo

This summer, I plan to spend five weeks in Togo with a group of six students from ENSEEIHT for a solidarity project. We will develop the project in collaboration with the association "Ada Togo", which will connect us with local young people to co-construct the initiative. Our objectives are:

  • Offer educational and fun activities
  • Raise awareness on topics such as contraception and sexually transmitted diseases
  • Support children living in precarious situations on a daily basis
  • Create a culturally enriching exchange for everyone

This project is important to us because it allows us to apply our skills to a meaningful cause, to experience another culture firsthand, and to develop personal and professional qualities such as adaptability, teamwork, and empathy. It also represents a unique opportunity to make a concrete contribution to the well-being and education of young people on site.
