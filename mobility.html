International Mobility

During our engineering degree programme, we are required to spend a semester abroad immersed in the country of our choice. We have the option of doing an internship or taking courses at a university and earning credits.

Taiwan

National Taiwan University (NTU) in Taiwan is my first wish for my exchange program. It offers many courses in its Computer Science & Information Engineering (CSIE) department, particularly in software engineering, a field that interests me greatly. Studying in Taiwan would also be a great opportunity to improve my Chinese and English. NTU is renowned for its international outlook and the diversity of its students, which allows you to discover new cultures and progress in an enriching environment.

Canada

Canada attracts me for its strong emphasis on research and development, as well as its multicultural environment. I hope to participate in projects that focus on sustainable technology and digital innovation.

Australia

Australia is appealing due to its cutting-edge tech hubs and vibrant start-up scene. I would like to learn how digital sciences are applied to industry and environmental solutions in a global context.
